    3. OH DEER

    In traditional lore, Santa Claus's sleigh is led by eight reindeer: Dasher, Dancer, Prancer, Vixen, Comet, Cupid, Dunder, and Bliksem. (Dunder and Bliksem are the dutch words for thunder and lightning.) Rudolph the Red Nosed Reindeer joined the team in the early 20th-century followed later by his brother Ralph the Infrared Nosed Reindeer.

    Updating this page today in Github in the browser. Got a bit lost for a while with pull requests, merging branches and gettin gh-pages to display the project. So quite a bit of searching with DuckDuckGo through GitHub help pages, StackOverflow answers and various walkthroughs.

    Most of the suggestions refer to command line routes and I mostly access GitHub through the browser. It seems to take me ages with GitHub to figure out how to do things.

    "Too many colourful snowflakes,and I'm not a reindeer."

    4. FOX

    In the night I stood and watched the clouds softly drifting. Mild from the West, around the Supermoon reflected sunlight. The damp and dripping hedgerow sparkling. From the shadows into the clearing light, a small furry animal, pale, slight hint of rust came towards me nose to ground. A cat? No. Tail too bushy, nose too pointy. It looked up at me then quietly turned around and left.

    Today I worked with images. I needed to know more about sizes for responsive web design and software and workflow to manage them. Then for practice improving this page, trying things. Naming and placing files and linking in the HTML, finding and trying hex colours for backgrounds in the CSS.
